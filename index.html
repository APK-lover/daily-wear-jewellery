Care Tips to Make Gold Jewelry Last Longer

Even the best quality gold needs proper care to last for years:

  • Avoid wearing gold while swimming or cleaning with chemicals.
  • Store pieces separately in soft pouches to avoid scratches.
  • Clean gently with mild soap and water every few weeks.
